Meet the Team

Meet the wonderful people behind the Mental Health Awareness Organization.

Salini A/P Ravinthiran

Salini is the visionary behind the concept of creating a mental health system. She has actively diagnosed and studied the impact of mental health illnesses since the onset of the Covid pandemic. Her research revealed that numerous individuals were silently struggling, often hidden from view. In her pursuit to make a difference, Salini reached out to her colleagues, Navveen and Suren, with the goal of establishing a server that could provide much-needed support to those in need.

Navveen Nair A/L Manoharan

Navveen is a 21-year-old student from the University of Technology, Malaysia (UTM). He is passionate about mental health and is eager to help create and improve the website. Navveen believes in using technology to make mental health resources more accessible to everyone.

Suren A/L Chandra Sekaran

Suren is a 21-year-old student from Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM). He is actively involved in charity work to raise awareness about mental health illnesses among people in Malaysia. Suren recognizes that mental health has been underlooked until the Covid pandemic highlighted its importance. His passion lies in encouraging those who are struggling to seek help and reach out as much as possible.

Our team is committed to making the world a better place by spreading awareness and providing support for mental health issues. Together, we believe in the power of community and compassion.
